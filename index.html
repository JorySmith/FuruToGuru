TA

  • Practice daily. Get your reps in. High quality. Real or sim/bar replay.
  • Check and Update HTF TTF TA every 5 minutes
  • Trend > Consolidation > Trend Resumption or Trend Reversal
  • TLs are the 3 widest points. Sup/res are most touches.
  • Current trend HTF TTF
  • Major Highs and Lows on HTF TTF. Major levels tend to hold on the first test.
  • Bull and bear TLs on HTF TTF
  • Bull and bear Pushes on HTF TTF. Especially 2-3 Pushes.
  • Bull and bear Entry locations on HTF TTF
  • Bull and bear Stops on HTF TTF
  • BO mode, strong trend, weak trend, TR consolidation
  • CVD: Buyers lifting offers vs Sellers hitting the bids
  • Strong moves and big bars usually get 2nd moves
  • Strong moves and big bars leave orders behind. They get filled during PBs.
  • A TL or level fails only with BO closes
  • Wicks do the damage. Closes tell the story.

Stops and Risk

  • Have Patience, let stops or targets decide exits, not your emotions
  • Bet/lose what on this trade? Including commissions and fees.
  • Bet your usual size, don't size up/nightengale, or you will be destroyed by the market
  • Multiple options or share size to allow partial exits?
  • Prior swing high low
  • Reasonable recent bar high low
  • Big bar close or 2RBO NEAR target
  • Reversal entry against your trade
  • Trail to new swing high low created by a BO or DB DT
  • Breakeven or EOD
  • TL BO reversal
  • 3rd push RC RBO
  • Scared? Stop far away? Size down
  • Let stop or target decide, can always get back in
  • Set alerts and targets, don't stare at PA, it's toxic and may cause you to not let your stop or targets decide.
  • If not using a hard stop and using a limit order stop, make it far beyond price to get out. This isn't the time to be stingy. You're wrong. Get out. Protect what you have.

After Action Reviews

  • Worst mistakes:
  • Trading too big
  • Reversing trade if wrong, not waiting for confirmation BO you're wrong
  • Not respecting original stop, stopping out emotionally
  • Not getting out at stop, big slippage using limit orders, use market orders if liquid or very wide limit orders
  • Not trading with TTF trend or waiting for TTF reversal
  • Not waiting for key HTF level to get in
  • Not waiting for your favorite trades or even know what they are and writing them down, with picture examples
