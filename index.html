  • TLs, bull and bear, current and potential future TLs

Big Picture Context, HTF

  • News? Massive attention?
  • Big move recently?
  • Great pattern or key level?
  • Overextended? Due for a big trend reversal?
  • 2nd/3rd push? Trend might need 50%-75% retracement before trend resumption

Pattern, TTF

  • New Trend, Trend Resumption, Trend Reversal, or Trading Range.
  • Is this a TL BO PB to DB DT BOPB HNS RBO or 2RBO? Trust it
  • A TL BO new trend is the priority bias unless PB to BOPB and HNS fail stop out close or PB test then bounce fails
  • BO spike to strong trend to weaker trend
  • BO spike close or BO spike PB RBO or wait for PB to test close RBO/2RBO
  • BO close fail stop out
  • Test RBO/2RBO fails stop out close
  • TL BO close
  • TL BO close to DB DT BOPB TL test close RBO/2RBO weaker trend continuation
  • DB DT BO close
  • Key level test bounce to DB DT BOPB RBO/2RBO failure
  • TL test close RBO/2RBO trend continuation
  • DB DT test close RBO/2RBO trend continuation
  • BOPB test close RBO/2RBO trend continuation
  • HNS or RBO test close RBO/2RBO
  • PB RC RBO 2RBO 3RBO Wedge reversal
  • Pattern BO fails or Test fails, stop out close or 2RBO stop out, trapped traders
