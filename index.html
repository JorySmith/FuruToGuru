Technical Analysis (TA)

  • Check and Update TA every 5 bars on TTF and HTF
  • Price moves from line to line, OHLC
  • TLs are the 3 widest points
  • Sup/res have one touch or the most touches
  • TLs, bull and bear, current and potential future TLs
  • DBs DTs, major highs and Lows. Major levels tend to hold on the first test.
  • Tests, TL BOPB HNS RBO
  • Pushes
  • Entries
  • Stops
  • Practice daily. Get your reps in. Real or sim, bar replay.
  • CVD: Buyers lifting offers vs Sellers hitting the bids
  • Strong moves and big bars usually get 2nd moves
  • Strong moves and big bars leave orders behind, they get filled during PBs.
  • A TL or level fails only with a BO close
  • Wicks do the damage. Closes tell the story.

Big Picture Context, HTF

  • News? Massive attention?
  • Big move recently?
  • Great pattern or key level?
  • Overextended? Due for a big trend reversal?
  • 2nd/3rd push? Trend might need 50%-75% retracement before trend resumption

Pattern, TTF

  • New Trend, Trend Resumption, Trend Reversal, or Trading Range.
  • BO spike to strong trend to weaker trend
  • BO spike, BO spike PB RBO or PB to test
  • TL Trend
  • TL BO
  • TL BO to DB DT BOPB TL weaker trend continuation
  • DB DT
  • BOPB
  • HNS or RBO
  • PB RC RBO 2RBO 3RBO Wedge
  • Pattern test or BO fails stops out, trapped traders
