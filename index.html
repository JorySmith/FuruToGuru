Technical Analysis (TA)

  • Logarithmic charts, OHLC, bias closes for exits, highs lows for entries
  • Update TA at least every 5 bars, key levels, DTL UTL, on ALL timeframes
  • Update stops and targets on the chart after BOs
  • Price moves from line to line, OHLC
  • UTL DTL are the 3 widest points
  • Sup/res have one touch or the most touches
  • Current UTL DTL, potential future UTL DTL
  • DBs DTs, major highs and Lows. Major levels tend to hold on the first test.
  • Tests of UTL DTL BOPB HNS RBO DB DT
  • Pushes
  • Entries
  • Stops
  • Practice daily. Get your reps in. Real or sim, bar replay.
  • Volume analysis, hitting the bid sell volume vs lifting offers buy volume
  • Strong moves and big bars usually get 2nd moves
  • Strong moves and big bars leave orders behind, they get filled during PBs.
  • UTL DTL or key level fails only with a BO close
  • Wicks do the damage. Closes tell the story.

HTF Pattern, Big Picture Context, Catalyst

  • News? Massive attention?
  • Big move recently?
  • Great pattern or key level?
  • Overextended? Due for a big trend reversal?
  • 2nd/3rd push? Trend might need 50%-75% retracement before UTL DTL resumption

TTF Pattern

  • BO, PB, Retracement, Key level Test, New Trend, Trend Resumption, Trend Reversal, or Trading Range.
  • Key levels: DB DT UTL DTL HNS RBO BOPB
  • Key level BO close or TL BO close? BO close PB RBO? Wait for PB to key level test?
  • Key level test RBO? Wait for 2nd entry 2RBO?
  • 2nd/3rd push PB RBO or 3rd push trend RBO?
  • BO fail RBO? Key level test fail BO close?
  • Overextended gap and crap, or RBO 2RBO 3RBO
  • UTL DTL BO new trend, trust it unless PB to BOPB and HNS fail stop out close or PB test bounce fails at DT DB TL
  • Bull bear BO spike > strong trend TL > weaker trend TL
  • Bull bear BO spike close
  • Bull bear BO spike close PB RBO or wait for PB to test close RBO/2RBO
  • Bull bear BO close fail stop out
  • UTL DTL BO close
  • UTL DTL BO close fail RBO 2RBO
  • UTL DTL BO close PB RBO
  • UTL DTL BO close PB to BOPB HNS RBO DB DT test close RBO 2RBO new trend continuation
  • UTL DTL BO close Push to DB DT BOPB TL test close RBO 2RBO weaker trend continuation
  • Test RBO/2RBO fails stop out close
  • DB DT BO close
  • UTL DTL BOPB HNS DB DT test close bounce failure at DB DT BOPB RBO/2RBO
  • UTL DTL test close RBO 2RBO trend continuation
  • DB DT test close RBO 2RBO trend continuation
  • BOPB test close RBO 2RBO trend continuation
  • HNS or RBO test close RBO 2RBO
  • PB RC RBO 2RBO 3RBO Wedge reversal
  • Pattern entry fails stop out close or RBO 2RBO stop out, trapped traders

After Action Reviews (AAR)

  • HTF pattern, TTF pattern, entry, stop, trailed stops, targets, lessons learned
  • Worst mistakes:
  • Not updating UTLs and DTLs every 5 bars
  • Before entry, not double-checking order, DTE, right contract, near EOD or weekend.
  • Not trading with HTF pattern bias on the TTF. Scalp if not, but focus should really be on joining the HTF pattern.
  • Trading too big, emotional, not the chart
  • Not respecting the stop or trailed stop, stopping out emotionally
  • Not getting out at stop, being stingy with limit orders
  • Not waiting for TTF to match up with HTF bias
  • Not taking a 2RBO entry over a bad signal bar, especially at a key level test
